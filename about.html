Watoto Kumi children's home was started in 1990 June
and it started with only ten kids. As the time goes

We are situated at Mombasa road Njori plaza along Angata lane.

At our home we do care for the children who have are victims of abused, abandoned,neglets and orphans too.
We do provive a range of residential services, educational services like massonry, computer studies, homescience, salonist skills and carpentry.
